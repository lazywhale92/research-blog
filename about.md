This blog is an MVP publication channel for paper-like technical notes. It is not a private notebook dump, portfolio theme, or generic AI content stream.

## Editorial contract

Each real note should make its claim structure visible:

1. What problem is being framed?
2. What method or experiment was used?
3. What evidence was observed?
4. What limitations remain?
5. What can another reader reproduce from public artifacts?

## Public boundary

Only public-safe content belongs here. Private drafts, raw logs, private conversations, unreleased company context, credentials, internal URLs, and confidential experiment traces stay outside this repository.
